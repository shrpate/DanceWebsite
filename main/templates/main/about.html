Our Commitment

Ananta Dance Academy is committed to offering the very best in Adult, Kids and Teen dance class instruction in a warm and welcoming environment. We provide our students with a place and space to grow: uniquely, individually, expressively, technically, passionately and with excellence. We offer instruction in a diverse and extensive dance repertoire from Ballet to Hip Hop to Contemporary to Ballroom. We are an accredited Ballroom Dance Academy, under the CDF, DVIDA and ISTD programs.
